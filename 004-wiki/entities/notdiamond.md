# NotDiamond

A routing service that powers [[concepts/auto_router|OpenRouter's Auto Router]] by analyzing prompts and selecting the optimal model from a curated pool based on prompt complexity, task type, and model capabilities. ^[raw/document/openrouter/openrouter-026-guides-routing-routers-auto-router-2026-04-29.md]

- Powers the Auto Router (`openrouter/auto`) on [[entities/openrouter|OpenRouter]] ^[raw/document/openrouter/openrouter-026-guides-routing-routers-auto-router-2026-04-29.md]
