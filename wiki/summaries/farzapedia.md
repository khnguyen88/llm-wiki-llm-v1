# Farzapedia

## Key Points

- Farzapedia is Farza's personal wiki, cited by Karpathy as a good example of the LLM Wiki pattern ^[raw/articles/farzapedia.md:1]
- The LLM Wiki approach to personalization has four key advantages over implicit AI learning: Explicit, Yours, File over app, and BYOAI ^[raw/articles/farzapedia.md:4-9]
- The Explicit property means memory artifacts are navigable — you can see exactly what the AI does and does not know ^[raw/articles/farzapedia.md:6]
- The Yours property means data stays on your local computer, not locked in a provider's system ^[raw/articles/farzapedia.md:7]
- The File over app property means data lives in universal formats (images, markdown), making it interoperable with any tool or CLI ^[raw/articles/farzapedia.md:8]
- The BYOAI property means you can use any AI to process the information, including finetuning an open source model on your wiki ^[raw/articles/farzapedia.md:9]
- Karpathy considers "agent proficiency" a core skill of the 21st century ^[raw/articles/farzapedia.md:13]

## Quotes

- "This approach to personalization puts _you_ in full control. The data is yours. In Universal formats. Explicit and inspectable. Use whatever AI you want over it, keep the AI companies on their toes!" ^[raw/articles/farzapedia.md:11]
- "'agent proficiency' is a CORE SKILL of the 21st century. These are extremely powerful tools - they speak English and they do all the computer stuff for you." ^[raw/articles/farzapedia.md:13]
