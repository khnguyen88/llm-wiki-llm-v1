# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher and educator who proposed the LLM Wiki pattern — using LLMs to build personal knowledge bases instead of traditional RAG.

## Key Contributions

- Authored the LLM Wiki gist describing the three-layer architecture (raw sources, wiki, schema) and three operations (ingest, query, lint)
- Advocates for "file over app" and "BYOAI" principles in knowledge management
- Demonstrated the approach with a research wiki of ~100 articles and ~400K words
- Connected the pattern to Vannevar Bush's Memex concept from 1945
