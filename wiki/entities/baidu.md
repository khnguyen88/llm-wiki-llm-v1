# Baidu

Chinese technology company and AI research organization, developer of the ERNIE (Enhanced Representation through kNowledge IntEgration) family of large language models. Baidu operates the ERNIE Bot chatbot platform and AI Studio for model experimentation. ^[raw/articles/baidu-ernie-5.1-0508-release.md]

## Key Facts

- Developer of the ERNIE model family (ERNIE 5.0, ERNIE 5.1) ^[raw/articles/baidu-ernie-5.1-0508-release.md]
- Built a disaggregated RL infrastructure on PaddlePaddle for training ERNIE models ^[raw/articles/baidu-ernie-5.1-0508-release.md]
- ERNIE 5.1 ranked 4th globally and 1st among Chinese models on Arena Search leaderboard (May 2026) ^[raw/articles/baidu-ernie-5.1-0508-release.md]
- ERNIE 5.1 is being rolled out on over ten creative production agent platforms ^[raw/articles/baidu-ernie-5.1-0508-release.md]
