# BYOAI (Bring Your Own AI)

The principle that you can use any LLM to "plug into" your knowledge base — Claude, Codex, OpenCode, or any other. The wiki is LLM-agnostic.

## Implications

- No vendor lock-in: your data and schema are portable
- Competition-friendly: keep AI companies on their toes by switching freely
- Future-proof: as better LLMs emerge, you can adopt them without migrating data
- Extreme possibility: finetune an open-source model on your wiki so it "knows" you in its weights, not just in context
