# Memex

Vannevar Bush's 1945 vision of a personal, curated knowledge store with associative trails between documents — a conceptual ancestor of the LLM Wiki pattern.

## The Connection

Bush's Memex was closer to the LLM Wiki than to what the web became: private, actively curated, with connections between documents as valuable as the documents themselves. The problem Bush couldn't solve was who does the maintenance. The LLM handles that.
